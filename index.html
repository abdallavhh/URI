About URI – Suez Canal Branch

URI – Undergraduate Research Initiative | Suez Canal Branch is a university student initiative that aims to empower students to participate in practical research projects, develop their academic, research, and leadership skills, and provide opportunities for real research projects and training programs at Suez Canal University.

Our Objectives

Public Relations (PR)

Manage campaigns and student communication to build strong community connections.

Media

Design, photography, and content production to showcase our initiatives effectively.

Human Resource (HR)

Manage people in our organization, including hiring, training, development, and member relations.

Faculty Ambassadors (FA)

Represent their college by supporting events, guiding students, and promoting the college's image.
