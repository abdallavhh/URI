About URI – Suez Canal Branch

URI – Undergraduate Research Initiative | Suez Canal Branch is a university student initiative that aims to empower students to participate in practical research projects, develop their academic, research, and leadership skills, and provide opportunities for real research projects and training programs at Suez Canal University.

Our Objectives

We focus on developing key areas that prepare students for successful research careers and academic excellence.

Research Skills

Enhance students' research and leadership abilities through hands-on experience and mentorship programs.

Academic Initiatives

Promote academic initiatives and community projects that make a real impact on society and education.

Hands-on Training

Offer comprehensive training programs such as SRT, RL, and RS for practical learning experiences.

Networking

Build strong networks between students, researchers, and academic initiatives for collaborative growth.

Our Programs

Join our comprehensive training programs designed to develop your research skills and academic expertise through practical experience.

SRT – Scientific Research Training

Practical training in scientific research for undergraduate students, providing real-world research experience and hands-on laboratory skills development.

RL – Research Leader

Preparing students to become research leaders and manage projects effectively in academic settings while developing essential leadership capabilities.

RS – Research School

Learn research methodologies in depth to conduct academic research effectively and professionally with comprehensive theoretical foundation.
