About URI – Suez Canal Branch

URI – Undergraduate Research Initiative | Suez Canal Branch is a university student initiative that aims to empower students to participate in practical research projects, develop their academic, research, and leadership skills, and provide opportunities for real research projects and training programs at Suez Canal University.

Our Objectives

Research Skills

Enhance students' research and leadership abilities.

Academic Initiatives

Promote academic initiatives and community projects.

Hands-on Training

Offer hands-on training programs such as SRT, RL, and RS.

Networking

Build a network between students, researchers, and academic initiatives.

Our Programs

SRT – Scientific Research Training

Practical training in scientific research for undergraduate students.

RL – Research Leader

Preparing students to become research leaders and manage projects.

RS – Research School

Learn research methodologies in depth to conduct academic research effectively.
